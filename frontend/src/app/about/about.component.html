About Us Page

It is a system by which students can appear in a quiz from anywhere of the world where there is no interaction between pencil and paper rather interaction between computer and human being.

we provide just a platform to be conduction online exam of enrolled students under any course in a department of any institute.

Main purpose of the project deals with providing facility of solving quiz online. Teachers of respective subjects can upload questions. Lab faculties also upload the programming questions. Students can solve those in given time. Students can see their result.

The system will determine the student's performance depending on his/her score. The system will show result after the examination is finished and also be able to provide feedback according to the result.

Administrative control of the whole system is provided. A teacher has control in the question bank and is suppose to make schedule for quiz. Teachers can use the OQES for evaluating students effectively, efficiently and perfectly.
